Finding Your Game

1

Go to your state page

Click your state on the map or navigate directly e.g. findmymahjgame.com/florida. Every state has its own page with players, events and venues.

2

Search by city or zip code

Use the city dropdown or enter your zip code and select your distance range 5, 10, 25 or 50 miles. Results update instantly.

3

Browse players, events and venues

Switch between the Players, Events and Where to Play tabs to find exactly what you’re looking for.

4

Connect & play!

Click Connect on a player card, RSVP to an event, or visit a venue. It’s always free for players.

List Yourself as a Player

1

Click “Create My Listing”

On any state page or from the map on the homepage.

2

Fill in your info

Your city, skill level, preferred schedule and a short bio. No last name or personal contact info required.

3

Go live!

Your listing appears on your state page and players nearby can connect with you. Always free.

Player listings are always 100% free. Find My Mahj Game will never charge players to find or list a game.
